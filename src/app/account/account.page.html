Change Password

To change to your password, input your Current Password then set and verify a New Password in both fields. You cannot change your User Name.

 User Name Current Password New Password Confirm New Password
  • 6 characters minimum
  • 1 minimum upper-case character (A-Z)
  • 1 minimum lower-case character (a-z)
  • 1 minimum numeric character (0-9)
  • 3 maximum identical sequential characters
  • 6 maximum characters from user name
  • 6 maximum characters from previous password
  • 6 previous passwords may not be re-used
  • 2 password change(s) per 1 hour(s)
  • Can not be in the common/restricted list
